About the Game

LiminalSpaces VR is a VR experience like no other, immersing you in the unsettling world of liminal spaces. Inspired by the backrooms concept, this game takes you through endless hallways, empty rooms, and eerie environments that feel both familiar and strange.

Game Features

  • Immersive VR experience
  • Explore various backrooms levels
  • Unsettling atmosphere and sound design
  • Interactive puzzles and challenges
  • Available on Meta/Oculus
